About Me

Hello 👋, i am Aditya Wahyu Santoso and MadByAd is my developer name which stands for Made By Aditya! I am a student who excels at programming and creative arts. The name MadByAd i choose because it is simple and sound russian and techy, in my opinion. The color black and white i choose because black represents the background of my code while white is my digital canvas or the result of it, pretty interesting philosophy right? I am an Indonesian programmer who mostly make games but i can also code website and as a student i like and love to be creative

I started programming 🧑‍💻 in late 2019. In the beginning i wanted to become a roblox developer and started learning Roblox Game Development, i did not manage to make any successfull game though i learn a lot. Eventually i leveled up my skills and started other kinds of programming, like actual Game Development using the Godot Game Engine and Web development and design. Eventually i also learned Blender and Krita which allows me to create 3D and 2D art. This digital medium is what i find best to pour in my creative idea ✨

I have made several games, design, and artwork that i have published and shared on my various social media and itch.io primarily the place for where i share my games, my best game includes: Scary Corporate Life Simulator, Pumpkin Nights, Bara Basic
